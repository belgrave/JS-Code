Policies on Waiting Time at Airports

60 minutes are included in the waiting time on all domestic and international flight arrivals on all airport pickups. After 60 minutes from the confirmed landing time, waiting time will be billed in increments of 30 minutes rounded up to the nearest half hour interval at the hourly rate for that vehicle plus gratuity, sales tax and STC.

Cancellation Policy

EP Car Service requires three (3) hours cancellation notice for all advanced reservations or the full charge applies. The full charge applies for all orders made less than three (3) hours before pick up time.
